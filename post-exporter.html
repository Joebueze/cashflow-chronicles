CashFlow Chronicle — Post Exporter
How to use after publishing a new post:
  1. Click "Refresh Posts" to load your latest posts
  2. Find your new post and click "Download Static File"
  3. Upload the downloaded file to your GitHub /posts/ folder
  4. Copy the sitemap entry and add it to your sitemap.xml
  5. Request indexing in Google Search Console
